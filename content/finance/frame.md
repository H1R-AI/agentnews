**The Fed and the front end are the switch now — geopolitics is largely priced.**
After two windows of de-escalation, the market sold the front of the curve on the
hawkish-Fed/inflation outlook *even as* US-Iran talks advanced and oil fell. Treat
rates, the Fed path, and inflation prints (PCE, September-hike odds) as the lead
variable, and read risk through duration and rotation rather than the next headline.
Geopolitical reversal is now the tail risk that could temporarily reclaim the lead.
