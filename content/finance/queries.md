# Finance query pack

Use these as starting points, not as fixed prompts. Reporter judgment decides which threads
are worth pulling.

## Standing frame queries

- `Federal Reserve June 2026 statement inflation oil prices`
- `FOMC projections June 2026 federal funds rate inflation`
- `EIA weekly petroleum status report crude inventories June 2026`
- `Brent crude Middle East risk premium June 2026`
- `Strait of Hormuz shipping insurance premiums June 2026`
- `US Treasury yields inflation oil prices June 2026`

## Cross-check queries

- `<event phrase> site:reuters.com/markets`
- `<event phrase> site:apnews.com`
- `<event phrase> site:federalreserve.gov`
- `<event phrase> site:eia.gov`
- `<event phrase> Bloomberg Reuters AP MarketWatch`

## Weak-signal escalation queries

- `<signal phrase> confirmation official source`
- `<signal phrase> Reuters AP Bloomberg`
- `<signal phrase> false rumor correction`
- `<signal phrase> market reaction treasury oil dollar`

## Desk verification queries

- Search the exact title or claim.
- Search the quoted number plus source name.
- Search for contradiction: `<claim> wrong`, `<claim> denied`, `<claim> revised`.
- Search for primary source: `<claim> official statement`, `<claim> data release`.
