# Finance source map

This is the reporter and desk source map. It is not exhaustive. The reporter should keep
expanding it as the beat matures.

## Primary / official

- Federal Reserve — FOMC statements, projections, minutes: https://www.federalreserve.gov/monetarypolicy/fomccalendars.htm
- Federal Reserve press releases: https://www.federalreserve.gov/newsevents/pressreleases.htm
- U.S. Treasury data: https://home.treasury.gov/policy-issues/financing-the-government/interest-rate-statistics
- EIA Weekly Petroleum Status Report: https://www.eia.gov/petroleum/supply/weekly/
- EIA weekly petroleum highlights PDF: https://ir.eia.gov/wpsr/wpsrsummary.pdf
- BLS CPI: https://www.bls.gov/cpi/
- BEA PCE / GDP: https://www.bea.gov/

## Major media / wires

- Reuters markets: https://www.reuters.com/markets/
- AP business: https://apnews.com/hub/business
- AP Middle East: https://apnews.com/hub/middle-east
- Bloomberg markets: https://www.bloomberg.com/markets
- Financial Times markets: https://www.ft.com/markets
- Wall Street Journal markets: https://www.wsj.com/finance

RSS radar for currently configured feeds lives in:

```text
content/finance/feeds.json
```

Use RSS to discover candidate leads quickly, then use AI search and the original article
or primary source before writing a claim.

## Specialist / market context

- CME FedWatch: https://www.cmegroup.com/markets/interest-rates/cme-fedwatch-tool.html
- CME economic calendar: https://www.cmegroup.com/education/events/econoday.html
- Trading Economics calendar: https://tradingeconomics.com/calendar
- MarketWatch economy: https://www.marketwatch.com/economy-politics

## Community / weak-signal surfaces

- Curated X lists: Fed watchers, oil traders, rates strategists, shipping analysts.
- Reddit: r/investing, r/geopolitics, r/economics. Use as `🔵 signal` unless corroborated.
- Hacker News only for tech/AI market spillovers, not for macro confirmation.

## Desk note

Prefer direct artifacts over homepages. A homepage is acceptable as a starting point, not as
final evidence for a specific claim.
